Ranking Units:Neighbouring Score

  • N-Zero:Set with element Considering District

  • N-Two: Set of distrcits sharing border with N-Zero districts
  • N-Zero Score: Total cases of Considering District
  • N-One Score: Summation of Total cases of districts where districts are union set of N-Zero and N-One
  • N-Two Score: Summation of Total cases of districts where districts are union set of N-Zero, N-One and N-Two
